TwoCars

Feb 09, 2017

A clone of the 2 Cars game in C++14/SFML TwoCars is a simple 2D endless runner where you simultaneously control two cars while taking all the circles and avoiding all triangles to keep running. How much can you score ?

Keybindings: F/J to change lane of the left/right car
